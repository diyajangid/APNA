About Founder

Aman Dhattarwal

Aman Dhattarwal is one of the most famous YouTubers, motivational speakers, and educators. He started his first channel on 11 December 2014, and since then, he has been helping students achieve their educational goals.

Aman was born on 22 September 1997 in a village in Rajasthan. Despite facing financial challenges, he excelled academically and moved to Delhi due to his father's job transfer. Aman celebrates his birthday on 4 March in memory of his brother.

Aman's dedication to education and his ability to inspire others have made him a beloved figure in the educational community. He continues to create content that empowers students to succeed.
